Shrine of Nameless Stars
Daniel Ignacio (aka dkaism) is a Filipino-Canadian artist from Toronto, Canada. After graduating from the University of Toronto in 2014, he taught himself the ways of the digital art media. In 2018, he became a freelance professional artist and has been working with independent clients and partners from around the world since. Through his work he likes to explore surrealism and fantasy genres and connect them to more contemporary fiction genres such as slice-of-life, solarpunk, cyberpunk, and dark academia.
