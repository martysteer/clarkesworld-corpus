Launch Day
Cristi Balanescu is a Romanian self-taught artist with a strong technical background. He's been working in the entertainment industry for over 6 years, and has gone form the menu artist to concept artist to Art Director. On the side he also did freelance work for projects like Lord of the Rings LCG, A Game of Thrones LCG, Warhammer Invasion, Call of Cthulhu LCG and Star Wars: The Card Game.
