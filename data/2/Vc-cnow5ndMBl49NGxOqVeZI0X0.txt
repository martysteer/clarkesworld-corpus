2013 Chesley Award Nominee for Best Cover Illustration: Magazine
Martin Faragasso is a self-taught illustrator from Argentina. He studied graphic design and animation, but soon he dropped out of college to start working in the motion design industry. Since then, he has been working in animation, modeling, design, concept design, matte painting and many different areas of the animation film process. In his spare time he enjoys painting and drawing, as they have always been his true passion. He aspires to become a full-time illustrator and concept artist. Through the different areas of traditional and digital art, whether they are animation or painting, Martin always tries to find new ways to break his own limitations and accomplish something truly unique.
