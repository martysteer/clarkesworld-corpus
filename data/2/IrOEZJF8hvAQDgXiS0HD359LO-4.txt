Alexander “Minze” Thümler is a german Concept Artist and Illustrator. After graduating with a bachelor’s degree in design in 2014, he started working in the entertainment industry. His portfolio includes design works for animated movies, cover artwork, game art, and more created with digital painting tools such as Photoshop, ProCreate, and the 3D software Blender.
Minze’s is streaming his personal and some commercial work on Twitch, sharing his process with others and creating a co-working atmosphere. In his spare time he love’s creating architecture and nature studies on location with a digital tablet as well as traditional drawing techniques. For a few years he’s been developing a fantasy graphic novel based on his bachelor’s thesis.
