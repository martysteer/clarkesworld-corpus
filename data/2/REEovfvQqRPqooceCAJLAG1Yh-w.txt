Colie Wertz is a freelance concept artist for hire working out of SanFrancisco, California. His interest in vehicles comes from an architectural background and a love of motorsport and aviation. He has worked in the film Industry for over twenty years as a modeler, viewpainter, mattePainter, compositor, and illustrator. He has worked on films from Star Wars:Episode 1: Phantom Menace to Rogue One: a Star Wars Story and The Mandalorian. Throw in some Transformers and Marvel movies to boot! He has drawn all his life, and continues to do so every day.
