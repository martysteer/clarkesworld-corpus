Natalie Shau is a Lithuanian CG artist currently living in London. She is comfortable working in formats as varied as illustration and photography, and music labels like Century Media, Nuclear Blast, and Black Lotus have been quick to utilize her talents. Natalie's work has also been featured in Imagine FX, Elegy #41, Photo Art, IEVA Magazine, Aboba, Le Calepin Jaune fanzine and for the cover of Fantasy Magazine issue #4.
