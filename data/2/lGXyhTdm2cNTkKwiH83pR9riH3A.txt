After being an Art Director at Zootfly for almost two years, Blaz recently joined the staff of Actalogic Game Studio. He is currently attending the Academy for Fine Arts and Design in Ljubljana and also working as a freelance illustrator. His most recent project is the Siddharta Saga, for the Slovenian rock band, Siddharta. This project involves illustrations to complement the story told in their upcoming CD as well all the merchandising materials to follow.
