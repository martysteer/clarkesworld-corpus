Aime studied at Emile Cohl art school in France and, after graduating, immediately entered video game field. This field allows her a choice of projects she enjoys and the opportunity to work as part of a team. She also tries to keep time for her own drawings.
Attracted by all that is graphic and modern, she tries to create rich and original universes... universes where giant nuclear robots, muscle cars and super heroes are kings.
