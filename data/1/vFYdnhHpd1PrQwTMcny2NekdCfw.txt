Serj is an art college graduate with an degree in Decoration and Interior Design. He has been drawing since he was four years old and has always had a passion for drawing fantasy, horror and sci-fi oriented stuff. As an active participant in art community sites like deviantArt, Sieg broadened his knowledge and interest in art, particularly digital art.
An avid gamer and industry aficionado, he hopes to someday find himself working in the field. "I never stop conceptualizing all sorts of characters, monsters and odd creatures, including worlds and environments."
