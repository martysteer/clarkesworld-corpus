Cover Art
Awe at Thistledown
Angel Nieves began working with visual effects in 1995 when he incorporated 3D images into his graphic design work for the advertising industry. Since then Angel has created models, textures, lighting and animation for a variety of projects in the broadcast, film and gaming industries. He is also the author of Lightwave v9 Texturing and has written tutorials for a number of websites and magazines.
"Awe at Thistledown" was inspired by Eon by Greg Bear.
