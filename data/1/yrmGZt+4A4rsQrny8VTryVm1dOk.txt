Cover Art
Off Road
2012 Finalist: Chesley Award for Best Magazine Cover
Facundo is a 24-year-old graphic design student from Argentina and currently working as a web designer/administrator of several websites. He has been drawing since he was very little, initially favoring traditional art and later experimenting with pencil, acrylic, and pastels. Three years ago, his attention turned to the digital art world. Inspired by talented artist such as Martin McKenna, Ryan Church, and Dermot Power, he started developing his photo manipulation, painting and airbrushing skills. He enjoys creating science fiction and fantasy illustrations and seeks to portray the most beautiful things from this world and his imagination.
