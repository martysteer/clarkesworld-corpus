Plains of Forgotten Hopes
Viko Menezes is a freelance illustrator and concept artist based in Brazil. The majority of his work is produced digitally, using a mix of 2D and 3D techiques, to create science fiction and fantasy art for a variety of clients. Recently he's been developing a personal project, The Last Drifter, a series of illustrations that follow a mysterious protagonist on her quests on a exotic world.
