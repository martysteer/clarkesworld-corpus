John Barry Ballaran is a freelance concept artist and illustrator based in the Philippines. He has been freelancing since graduation in 2005, taking up projects from all corners of the art industry.
His art can be seen in advertising, film, comics, book covers and a few casual game titles in the market. He specializes in 2D digital art, employing 3D software to aid his illustrations when needed.
