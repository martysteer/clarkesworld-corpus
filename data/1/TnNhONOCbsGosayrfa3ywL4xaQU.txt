Garden
Alejandro Miguel Burdisio, artistically known as Burda, was born in the city of Córdoba. A self-taught artist, he’s been drawing, painting, and illustrating professionally since he was nineteen years old. With more than thirty years of experience as an illustrator, he works for local and foreign companies through the graphics studio that bares his name.
His longtime dedication to fantasy art currently allows him to participate in animation productions, short films, video games, and motion pictures as a concept artist for production companies around the world. In his travels, he gives training talks and dissertations at different universities in his country and throughout Latin America.
