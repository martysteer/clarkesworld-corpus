Cover Art
Dead Space Girl
Sergio Diaz is 31-year-old artist from Buenos Aires. He has been a character illustrator, 3D modeler and texture artist for more than ten years. His started his career as a 3D artist was making architectonic visualizations, but quickly switched working for an advertising agency where he made several local and international TV commercials. After three years, he made the decision to work freelance, making illustrations, character designs, character modeling, textures, compositions, matte paintings, etc. In 2011, he was hired by a production company to make 3D modeling, UVS, blend shapes, textures and character shading for an upcoming animated movie.
