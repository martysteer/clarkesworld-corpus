2013 Chesley Award Nominee for Best Cover Illustration: Magazine
Ken Barthelmey was born in Luxembourg in 1989. He has always been passionate about art and began drawing from the moment he was old enough to hold a pencil. He is fascinated by the way a painting can tell a story. Most recently, his focus has been on creating creatures and characters, but he also has a passion for objects and environments. His goal is to create cool and interesting concepts that are realistic and believable.
